Vloverz FoodWorks Limited (the Company) is a Jubilant Bhartia Group Company. The Company was incorporated in 1995 and initiated operations in 1996. The Company got listed on the Indian bourses in February 2010. Mr. Shyam S. Bhartia, Mr. Hari S. Bhartia and Jubilant Consumer Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Jubilant Fresh Pvt. Ltd.) are the Promoters of the Company. The Company & its subsidiary operates Loverz's Pizza brand with the exclusive rights for India,Russia, Nepal, Bangladesh and Sri Lanka. The Company is market leader in the pizza segment with a network of 1,249 loverz Pizza restaurants across 276 cities in India (as on april 30, 2020).

Over the period since 1996, Loverz Pizza India has remained focused on delivering great tasting Pizzas and sides, superior quality, exceptional guest care and value for money offerings.
